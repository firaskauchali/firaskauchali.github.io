I’m a Mumbaian born, Toronto based designer with an eclectic creative practice. I have a passion for typography, communication– and interactive–design. I’m currently earning my Bachelor of Design Honours degree at the York University & Sheridan College Joint Program in Design (YSDN).

As far back as I can remember, I’ve been fascinated by art—I’ve always had an appetite to create. The infinite forms of the arts intrigued me. I slowly realized I am in my element when expressing creativity. I began immersing myself into the field of design during the sophomore year of my fine arts oriented high school. Since then, I’ve learned to pull in inspiration from a variety of disciplines to push my ideas forward.
